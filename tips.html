Oil For Cutting Tools

Use cooking oil as a protectant on any edged tools you plan to use for cooking. Using another protectant like WD-40 or Hoppes #9 can inadverntantly poison any food it comes in contact with.

Military Surplus?

Military surplus equipment ususally provides a cheap, tough and dummy-proof solution for many needs/wants while out in the bush. The most consistent trade-off is weight. More expensive civilian designed gear will usually weigh less than rugged military gear designed for individuals with double digit IQ's.
